Ingredients:
 - 2 cups miso
 - 2 cups fish stock
 - 1 cup boiling water
 - 1/3 pack of tofu, cut into 1/3-inch cubes
 - 1/2 long green onion, thinly sliced
 - 2-3 scallions 
 - 6-8 ounces of fresh spinach, washed and chopped

Directions:
1. in a medium saucepan, bring the fish soup stock to a boil
2. add chopped spinach and thick green onion cook unstil the greens are not too tender
3. Add tofu
4. Blend miso in the ladle of hot stock before adding it to the soup
5. Heat soup to just below a simmer over low heat
6. Ladle into bowl

Serve while hot!
